What does it do?

SQL Schema Explorer is a new tool for your SQL toolbox.

If you are a developer or DBA who spends a lot of time with relational databases then this tool will make your life easier and more fun.

  • Browse data, diagrams and schema information for your database in a simple web interface.
  • Give others self-serve access to data and schema information - share schema explorer on your network.
  • Quickly get to grips with unfamiliar database schema and the data hidden inside.
  • Stop writing JOIN statements and guessing foreign key columns just to answer simple data questions.
  • Help your team understand and improve your database schema by making it more visible.
  • Focus on the problem you are trying to solve instead of wasting effort with SSMS and T-SQL just to answer basic questions about your database schema and data.

Easy To Use

Setup

  1. Download schema explorer onto your computer.
  2. Connect to your database.
  3. Start browsing your schema and data.

Usage

  1. Point your browser at the generated website.
  2. Click around the schema and data, using the links on the pages to follow relationships and jump between related information.
