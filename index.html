A new tool for your SQL toolbox. If you are a developer or DBA who spends a lot of time with relational databases then this tool will make your life easier and more fun!

  • Browse data, diagrams and schema information for your database in a simple web interface.
  • Quickly get to grips with unfamiliar database schema and the data hidden inside.
  • Stop wasting time writing endless JOIN statements to get the data you want.
  • Answer data questions across related tables in just a few clicks without writing SQL.
  • Never again have to painstakingly create and lay out SSMS diagrams just to see how tables relate.
  • Give others self-serve access to often-requested data by sharing the website generated by schema explorer on your network.
  • Reply to questions about your database with a url instead of screenshots and spreadsheets attached to emails.
  • Once you try this against your own database you'll never want to be without it again!

Live Demo

Easy setup

  1. Download the schema explorer onto your computer or server.
  2. Connect to your database.
  3. Start browsing your schema and data.

Easy usage

  • Open the generated web page to view the list of tables in your database and useful information about your database schema.
  • Click a table in the diagram or table list to navigate to that table, you will then see:
    • Column definitions
    • Foreign keys, including inbound keys
    • The first 100 rows of data
  • Click on tables in the relationship diagram to jump to that chosen table.
  • Click the hyperlinked data to jump to the exact matching rows in related tables.
