How to use it

Click a table in the diagram to view all the information about that table and the first 100 rows of data.

The diagram now shows directly related tables, and the data view has hyperlinks for inbound and outbound foreign keys. Click related tables in the diagram or the links in the data view to jump straight to the relevant data in the related table.
